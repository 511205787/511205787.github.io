abstract: >-
  Phy-SSM is a general-purpose framework for long-term dynamics forecasting in noisy and irregularly sampled environments. It combines partial physics knowledge with state space models by decomposing system dynamics into known and unknown components, and further improves prediction quality through physics state regularization. Experiments on vehicle motion prediction, drone state prediction, and COVID-19 epidemiology forecasting show strong gains over prior methods in both interpolation and extrapolation settings.
