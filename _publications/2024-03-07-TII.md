abstract: >-
  This work studies temperature field reconstruction for lithium-ion battery packs under changing operating conditions. We propose a deep transfer operator learning method based on physics-informed adversarial networks, where transformer blocks capture spatiotemporal features, thermal partial differential equations improve physical consistency, and domain-adversarial transfer learning encourages domain-invariant representations. The resulting model reconstructs temperature fields accurately even in previously unseen scenarios and outperforms prior deep learning baselines under real-world energy storage conditions.
