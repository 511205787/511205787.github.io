abstract: >-
  VF-NODE accelerates Neural ODE training for irregularly sampled dynamical systems by replacing solver-heavy autoregressive training with a variational formulation based on global integrals. The method evaluates neural vector fields only at observed data points, greatly reducing the number of function evaluations and mitigating accumulated numerical errors. It further incorporates Filon's method and natural cubic spline regression to handle oscillatory integrals, noisy observations, and incomplete data, achieving major speedups while maintaining or improving accuracy.
