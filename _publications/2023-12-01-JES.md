abstract: >-
  This paper addresses battery temperature prediction across full life cycles, where aging and irreversible heat generation make accurate modeling difficult. Inspired by physics-informed neural networks, we develop an interpretable battery-informed neural network that integrates a battery physical model into the learning pipeline and further enhances performance with a multi-head attention mechanism. The method improves generalization and transferability over traditional data-driven approaches while supporting safer and more efficient battery operation.
