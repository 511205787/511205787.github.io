abstract: >-
  Fast-VF Neural ODE is a variational-formulation-based approach for accelerating the training of Neural ODEs on irregularly sampled dynamical systems. Instead of relying on adaptive-step numerical solvers throughout optimization, it reformulates training in a way that is substantially more efficient and introduces Filon's method to reduce the impact of oscillatory terms in the loss. Experiments show 10x to 100x faster training than baseline methods while preserving comparable predictive accuracy.
